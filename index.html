2. Meal Plan Options

Focus on soft consistency and minimal seasoning (no chili, minimal/no salt or sugar).

Breakfast (Carbs \& Protein)

  • Soft Idli or Dosa with mild Sambar.
  • Vegetable Upma (finely chopped veggies).
  • Besan or Moong Dal Cheela (Pancake).

Lunch (Main Nutrients)

  • Soft Roti with simple Moong/Masoor Dal and mashed vegetable.
  • Khichdi (Rice and Dal with Ghee).
  • Curd Rice or Mild Vegetable Pulao.

Snacks 1 \& 2 (Energy Boost)

  • Cut soft fruit (Mango, Banana, Pear, Papaya).
  • Plain Curd (Dahi) / 1-2 pieces of Paneer.
  • Sprouted Moong Chaat (mild) / Crushed nuts mixed with date paste.

3. 7-Day Dinner Rotation

Keep dinners light and easily digestible for better sleep.

Mon: Soft Moong Dal Khichdi

Tue: Vegetable Roti Roll (Mashed Paneer/Veggies)

Wed: Sweet Potato \& Masoor Dal Mash

Thu: Atta Cheela (with grated Lauki)

Fri: Curd Rice (Thayir Sadam)

Sat: Oats or Daliya Porridge (with fruit)

Sun: Besan Cheela (with Spinach)

Safety \& Flexibility Guidelines

  • Choking Safety is Mandatory: All food must be soft, mashed, or cut into tiny, manageable pieces. Round foods (like grapes or cherry tomatoes) **must be quartered**.
  • Encourage Independence: Allow the child to try self-feeding with their hands or a child-sized spoon to build **confidence and fine motor skills**.
  • Follow Cues: The timetable is a guide. Always follow the child's natural hunger and sleep signals. If they need to eat early or take a shorter nap, adjust the rest of the day accordingly.
  • Hydration: Offer a sippy cup of **water** multiple times a day, especially during active play and mealtimes.
